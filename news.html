Director Platform beta opening soon — manage.twirlpower.com

May 2026

We're excited to announce that the TwirlPower Director Platform is entering beta testing at manage.twirlpower.com.

The Director Platform is a dedicated app built specifically for competition directors — completely separate from the family and coach app. It gives directors everything they need to plan, build, and run a baton twirling competition from start to finish.

What's already built in the Director Platform:

  • Competition setup with full lifecycle management (Draft → Published → Registration → Live → Completed)
  • Public registration form — embeddable on your own website
  • Entries management — manual entry, online form, duet and group support
  • Visual schedule builder — drag events onto a lane board
  • Gyms and lanes management with configurable lane counts
  • Judge roster management
  • PDF and CSV schedule export
  • Public/Private competition visibility toggle
  • Competition listings auto-appear on directory.twirlpower.com and twirlpower.com

What's coming next: Results and scoring tab, entry fee collection via Stripe, conflict detection, real-time scoreboard, and judge scoring interface.

If you're a competition director interested in beta testing, visit manage.twirlpower.com to get started.

Launch

TwirlPower is live — open beta now available

April 2026

We're excited to announce that TwirlPower is now in open beta and free for all families, coaches, and competition directors to use.

TwirlPower is a web and mobile app built specifically for competitive baton twirling — tracking classification levels, competition results, and advancement progress across USTA, NBTA, TU, and DMA.

What's available right now:

  • Classification tracking with win counts and progress bars per org and event
  • Competition history with placements, scores, and scorecard uploads
  • Twirl Tracker — a competition-day dashboard that auto-activates
  • Coach accounts with full studio roster and advancement alerts
  • Club and studio directory with claim flow
  • Competition director accounts with public listings
  • Public directory at directory.twirlpower.com
  • PWA — installable on iOS and Android
  • Online competition registration — multi-event, duet, and group support
  • Event Planner — pre-plan events with set numbers before competition day

The app is completely free during open beta. We're actively building and improving based on feedback from the twirling community. Get started at app.twirlpower.com.
