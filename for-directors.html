How It Works

Listed in minutes. In front of every family already tracking results.

1

Apply for Director Access

Create a TwirlPower account and submit a quick verification request. Our team reviews and approves within 24 hours.

2

Create Your Competition

Add your competition name, date, location, org, and details. It publishes instantly to the directory and marketing site.

3

Reach Families & Coaches

Families using TwirlPower see your competition in the directory and can add it to their tracker. Coaches invite their twirlers directly from the app.
