Our Story

Built by a twirling family, for twirling families.

Twirling families have been tracking competition results on paper since the 1960s. Scorecards folded into manila folders. Notes in margins. Tabs on calendars. We built TwirlPower because we lived it — and we believed it was time to bring this sport into the modern era.

TwirlPower will always be free for families.

No paywalls. No subscriptions. If you ever find the app valuable enough to support, there's a way to do that — but only if you choose.

Why we made that promise. This sport runs on the people in it — the parents in the stands, the coaches in the gym, the families who drive across the state for a Saturday competition. We won't gate any of that behind a paywall. The tools a family needs to follow their twirler's journey should belong to every family, not just the ones who can pay for them.

That's why the core of TwirlPower — classification tracking, results history, advancement progress across USTA, NBTA, TU, DMA, AAU, and WTA — is free, and stays free. We're a twirling family ourselves, and we built the thing we wished we'd had.

What's next. The family app is just the beginning. We're building tools for the coaches who built this sport, and a competition platform that lets directors run their events with confidence — scheduling, scoring, and results that flow straight back to families. The home base for competitive baton twirling, for everyone in it.

Built by a twirling family, for twirling families. And free for your family — always.
