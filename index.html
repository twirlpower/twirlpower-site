Features

Built around how competitive
twirling actually works.

Not a generic sports tracker with baton slapped on it. Every feature exists because someone in this sport needed it.

📊

Classification Tracking

Finally, someone did the math for you.

Win counts, progress bars, and advancement thresholds — tracked per org, per event. USTA, NBTA, TU, and DMA all handled with their own rules.

🏅

Competition History

Every scorecard, every placement, every org — in one place forever.

Log placements, scores, all-catch results, and scorecard uploads. Build a complete competition record your twirler can look back on for years.

⚡

TwirlTracker

Competition day is chaotic enough. This isn't.

A competition-day dashboard that auto-activates on event day. See your twirler's events, classification status, and recent results in one clean view.

👥

Coach Roster & Alerts

Know before they have to tell you.

Coaches get a full studio roster with classification chips, advancement alerts, and the ability to invite twirlers to competitions directly in the app.

🏛️

Club & Studio Management

Your studio, on the map.

Claim your club on TwirlPower, manage members, add coaches, and appear in the public directory at directory.twirlpower.com.

📱

Works Everywhere

Add it to your home screen like an app — no app store, no download.

Works on iOS, Android, and desktop. Add to your home screen in seconds, and access your twirler's info even when the gym wifi doesn't cooperate.
