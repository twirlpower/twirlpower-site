Multi-Org Support

Every organization, one app

Each org has its own classification system, advancement rules, and event structure. TwirlPower handles all of them correctly.

USTA

United States Twirling Association

Novice → Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced. Win-count and judge recommendation tracking. CAS system for Compulsories and Movement Technique.

NBTA

National Baton Twirling Association

Novice → Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced. Final round wins only, protection rule included.

TU

Twirling Unlimited

Sp. Beginner through Advanced. Cumulative win tracking — all org wins count toward advancement.

DMA

Drum Majorettes of America

5 levels including Elite. Trophy-count model — Elite requires top-3 Nationals finishes.
