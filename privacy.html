1. Who We Are

TwirlPower is a web and mobile application for competitive baton twirling families, coaches, and competition directors. It is operated by OAKRAA, LLC, a Colorado limited liability company, doing business as TwirlPower ("TwirlPower," "we," "us," or "our").

TwirlPower is accessible at app.twirlpower.com, twirlpower.com, and directory.twirlpower.com.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use TwirlPower. By using TwirlPower, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, state of residence, and password when you create an account.
  • Twirler profiles: First name, date of birth (for age verification and classification purposes), organization memberships, and competition events.
  • Competition data: Competition names, dates, locations, placements, scores, all-catch results, and uploaded scorecards.
  • Coach information: Studio name, biography, state, organizations coached, and club affiliations.
  • Club and director information: Club name, location, owner details, and competition listings.
  • Co-guardian information: Name, email address, and relationship to the twirler when added by an account holder.
  • Communications: Messages, bug reports, and beta feedback you submit to us.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interactions within the app.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and referring URLs.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We do not currently collect information from third-party social media platforms or data brokers. If this changes, we will update this policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the TwirlPower application and services
  • Track classification levels, win counts, and advancement progress across organizations (USTA, NBTA, TU, DMA, AAU, WTA)
  • Enable coach-to-family connections and competition invitations
  • Display competition results and history within the app
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmations, advancement alerts, coach link requests, club notifications)
  • Display public-facing information in the TwirlPower directory (coaches, clubs, competitions) where you have opted in
  • Respond to your support requests, bug reports, and feedback
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for targeted advertising. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other TwirlPower Users

  • Coaches: When you link a twirler to a coach, that coach can view the twirler's classification levels, competition history, and advancement status.
  • Co-guardians: Authorized co-guardians you invite can view twirler data at the access level you assign (full access or read-only).
  • Public directory: Coach profiles and club listings that opt into directory visibility appear publicly at directory.twirlpower.com. Competitions created by verified directors appear publicly by default.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate TwirlPower, including:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Vercel — application hosting (vercel.com)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (resend.com)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for their own purposes.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TwirlPower, our users, or others.

4.4 Business Transfers

If TwirlPower or OAKRAA, LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's PostgreSQL database infrastructure, hosted in the United States. We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Role-based access controls limiting who can view sensitive data
  • Secure file storage for uploaded documents (scorecards, club claim documents)

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@twirlpower.com.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TwirlPower tracks competitive baton twirlers, many of whom are under 13 years old. We take our obligations under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) seriously.

6.1 How Children's Data Is Handled

Children under 13 do not create their own TwirlPower accounts. All accounts for minor twirlers are created and managed by a parent or legal guardian through a family account. The parent or guardian is the account holder and is responsible for all data entered on behalf of their child.

6.2 What Children's Data We Collect

Through family accounts, we collect the following information about minor twirlers:

  • First name
  • Date of birth (used for age-based classification purposes)
  • Competition history, placements, and scores
  • Organization memberships and classification levels

We do not collect full names, addresses, school information, photos, or any other personally identifying information about minor twirlers beyond what is listed above.

6.3 Parental Rights

As the account holder and parent/guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review the information we have collected about your child
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your child's information
  • Refuse further collection or use of your child's information by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@twirlpower.com.

6.4 No Direct Marketing to Children

We do not send marketing communications directly to minor twirlers. All communications are directed to the parent or guardian account holder.

7. Your Rights & Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You can review and update most of your account information directly within the TwirlPower app. For information you cannot update yourself, contact us at support@twirlpower.com.

7.2 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@twirlpower.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention).

7.3 Directory Visibility

Coaches can control whether their profile appears in the public directory through their coach profile settings. Clubs can control directory visibility through club settings.

7.4 Email Communications

Transactional emails (account alerts, advancement notifications, competition invites) are necessary for the service to function and cannot be opted out of while you have an active account. If we introduce marketing emails in the future, we will provide an unsubscribe option.

7.5 California Residents

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at support@twirlpower.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking

TwirlPower uses minimal cookies and local storage for essential functionality:

  • Authentication: Supabase session tokens to keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Local storage to remember UI preferences (such as dismissed notices)
  • PWA caching: Service worker cache for offline read-only functionality

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics platforms. We do not participate in cross-site tracking.

The public marketing site (twirlpower.com) and directory (directory.twirlpower.com) may use basic analytics to understand traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is used for this purpose.

9. Third-Party Services

TwirlPower integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Resend — resend.com/privacy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, though we have selected providers with strong privacy commitments.

  • Notify active users by email at least 14 days before changes take effect
  • For changes affecting children's data, obtain fresh parental consent where required by law

Your continued use of TwirlPower after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
