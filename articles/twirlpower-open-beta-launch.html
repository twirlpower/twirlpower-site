TwirlPower is live — open beta now available

April 2026 · By TwirlPower

We're excited to announce that TwirlPower is now in open beta and free for all families, coaches, and competition directors to use.

TwirlPower is a web and mobile app built specifically for competitive baton twirling — tracking classification levels, competition results, and advancement progress across USTA, NBTA, TU, and DMA.

What's available right now:

  • Classification tracking with win counts and progress bars per org and event
  • Competition history with placements, scores, and scorecard uploads
  • Twirl Tracker — a competition-day dashboard that auto-activates
  • Coach accounts with full studio roster and advancement alerts
  • Club and studio directory with claim flow
  • Competition director accounts with public listings
  • Public directory at directory.twirlpower.com
  • PWA — installable on iOS and Android
  • Online competition registration — multi-event, duet, and group support
  • Event Planner — pre-plan events with set numbers before competition day

The app is completely free during open beta. We're actively building and improving based on feedback from the twirling community. Get started at app.twirlpower.com.
