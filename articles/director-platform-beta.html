We're excited to announce that the TwirlPower Director Platform is entering beta testing at manage.twirlpower.com.

The Director Platform is a dedicated app built specifically for competition directors — completely separate from the family and coach app. It gives directors everything they need to plan, build, and run a baton twirling competition from start to finish.

What's already built in the Director Platform:

  • Competition setup with full lifecycle management (Draft → Published → Registration → Live → Completed)
  • Public registration form — embeddable on your own website
  • Entries management — manual entry, online form, duet and group support
  • Visual schedule builder — drag events onto a lane board
  • Gyms and lanes management with configurable lane counts
  • Judge roster management
  • PDF and CSV schedule export
  • Public/Private competition visibility toggle
  • Competition listings auto-appear on directory.twirlpower.com and twirlpower.com

What's coming next: Results and scoring tab, entry fee collection via Stripe, conflict detection, real-time scoreboard, and judge scoring interface.

If you're a competition director interested in beta testing, visit manage.twirlpower.com to get started.
