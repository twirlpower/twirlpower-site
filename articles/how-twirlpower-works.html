For Families

Stop guessing. Start knowing.

Every win, every level, every competition — in one place.

Step 1 — Create your account

Head to app.twirlpower.com and sign up. Add your twirler's profile — first name, date of birth, and which organizations they compete in (USTA, NBTA, TU, DMA — pick as many as apply). Takes 2 minutes.

Step 2 — Log your first competition

Open TwirlPower and add your results. Placement, score, event — whatever you have. The app tracks wins toward advancement automatically. No spreadsheets. No trying to remember.

Step 3 — Watch their classification build

See exactly where your twirler stands in each event and org — how many wins they have, how many until the next level, and what that history looks like over time.

Step 4 — Use TwirlTracker on competition day

On competition day, TwirlTracker activates automatically. See all your twirler's events, add results as they happen, and never lose track of where you are in the day.

For Coaches & Studios

Your whole roster. One view.

See exactly where every twirler stands — without asking their parents.

Step 1 — Create your coach account

Sign up at app.twirlpower.com and select "I'm a coach." Set up your profile with your studio name, state, and organizations you coach.

Step 2 — Build your roster

Find families already on TwirlPower and send a connection request. For families not yet on the platform, invite them by email — they'll get a link to join and connect automatically.

Step 3 — Track advancement across your studio

Your roster shows every twirler's current classification level, wins toward advancement, and which twirlers are ready to move up. No more keeping it all in your head.

Step 4 — Invite twirlers to competitions

Create competitions in TwirlPower and invite your twirlers directly. They'll see it in their family app and can confirm attendance.
